How to View Registrations

1

Login to Netlify

Go to app.netlify.com and log in to your account.

2

Select Your Site

Click on your SKYFIRE contest website in the dashboard.

3

Access Forms

Navigate to the "Forms" tab to see all submissions from the registration form.

4

View Details

Click on any submission to see full details including all form fields.

Registration Form Data Structure

Each registration submission will contain the following fields:

Basic Information

  • • name: Participant's full name
  • • email: Contact email address
  • • country: Country/region
  • • phone: Phone number (optional)
  • • company: Organization name (optional)

Contest Details

  • • participation_type: Individual or team
  • • team_members: Team member list (if applicable)
  • • power_level: Laser power specification
  • • source: How they heard about contest
  • • agreed: Terms acceptance
