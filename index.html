📦 T-shirt Production:

Once registration is confirmed, the organizers will begin creating T-shirts and send them to participants.

🎥 Video Submission Requirements:

After registering, participants need to create a video (5-8 minutes long). The video should explain the DIY process and showcase the machine's appearance, functionality, and cutting performance.

Since our evaluation is conducted through live streaming, please keep your video duration between 5-8 minutes to save time during the review process.
